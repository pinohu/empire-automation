# Non-Disclosure Agreement Template

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between [Your Entity Name] ("Disclosing Party") and [Other Party Name] ("Receiving Party") on [Date].

PURPOSE:
The parties wish to engage in discussions regarding [purpose of discussions] and may disclose confidential information to each other.

CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION:
Confidential Information includes all non-public, proprietary information disclosed by either party, including but not limited to:
- Business plans and strategies
- Financial information
- Customer lists
- Technical information
- Any information marked as confidential

OBLIGATIONS:
Receiving Party agrees to:
- Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties
- Use Confidential Information solely for the stated purpose
- Return or destroy Confidential Information upon request

EXCEPTIONS:
This Agreement does not apply to information that:
- Is publicly available
- Was already known to Receiving Party
- Is independently developed
- Is required to be disclosed by law

This Agreement shall remain in effect for [X] years from the date of execution.
